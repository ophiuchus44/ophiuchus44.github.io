Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a 'Public Profile'?

    You can't upload what you look like into your public facing profile. This means your public facing profile ends up being pictures like the car you're working on or the trail you like to run on. The idea is, share who you are without showing what you look like.

  • What is a 'Private Profile'?

    Once you exchange 5 messages between each other, a 'Let's meet' button will appear. If both people click that, then you will gain access to their private profile which HAS to be what they look like. We also count faces, so you don't need to figure out which person in the group photo you're talking to.
